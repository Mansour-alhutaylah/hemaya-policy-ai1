INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
Organization: Acme Company
Version: 1.0

1. PURPOSE
This policy outlines our commitment to information security. The organization takes security seriously and will protect its information assets.

2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all employees and contractors.

3. SECURITY PRINCIPLES
The organization follows best practices for cybersecurity. We are committed to keeping our systems secure and protecting data from unauthorized access.

4. ACCESS CONTROL
Employees must use passwords to access company systems. Access should be appropriate for each job role. Managers are responsible for ensuring their team follows this policy.

5. DATA PROTECTION
Company data must be treated with care. Sensitive information should not be shared with unauthorized parties. We comply with applicable data protection laws.

6. INCIDENT REPORTING
Security incidents should be reported to IT. The IT team will handle all security incidents and take appropriate action.

7. TRAINING
Employees will receive security awareness training. All staff are expected to follow this policy.

8. REVIEW
This policy will be reviewed periodically and updated as needed.

9. COMPLIANCE
All employees must comply with this policy. Violations may result in disciplinary action.
