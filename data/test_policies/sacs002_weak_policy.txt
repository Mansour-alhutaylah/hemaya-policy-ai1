THIRD PARTY CYBERSECURITY POLICY

1. General

Our organization takes cybersecurity seriously. We follow industry best practices and
have policies in place to protect information and systems. All employees are expected to
behave responsibly with technology resources.

2. Information Security

We have an information security program that addresses data protection. Sensitive
information is protected appropriately. Our systems are secured against unauthorized access.

3. Third Party Management

We work with third parties under agreements that include security requirements. Third
parties are expected to maintain adequate security controls. We review third party
relationships periodically.

4. Access Control

Access to systems is managed. Users have accounts and passwords. Administrative access is
limited to authorized personnel.

5. Incident Response

Security incidents are handled by our IT team. Issues are reported and investigated.

6. Compliance

We comply with applicable laws and regulations. Security controls are reviewed annually.
